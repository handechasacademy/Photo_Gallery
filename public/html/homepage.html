Welcome to my image gallery

This is a little image gallery filled with some of my favorite things.
The images are grouped into different categories, each one representing
something I love, so feel free to explore and click around.

Photos sourced from Unsplash and Pexels, used under their free licenses.

How to use the site

  • Scroll through the gallery to see all images as thumbnails
  • Click on any image to view it in a larger format
  • Click on the X on top right of the page or press ESC
    to exit the larger format and return to gallery
  • Use the category tabs to filter images
  • Use the search bar located at the end of
    the navigation bar to find images by tags
